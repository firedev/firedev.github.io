The way out

Guided awakening for those who feel they were born for something else.

This is what I do when not working on the next startup.

We go to the remote mountain, an inhabited island and other mind-blowing remote location around a full moon. No other people around.

We immerse ourselves in a psychedelic experience of deep meditation. Under supervision and with accommodation.

Properly done it’s a week-long trip. We go sight-seeing, head to the location, where we conduct the ceremony. Followed with integration and coaching sessions.
